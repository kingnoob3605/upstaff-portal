Excel 365 Skills Test

📊 About this Test

This test covers 5 sections of Excel knowledge drawn from a pool of questions:
📐 Formulae
SUM, IF, VLOOKUP & more
📋 General Topics
Navigation, shortcuts, basics
🖨️ Formatting & Printing
Cells, styles, print layout
📈 Charts
Chart types, editing, formatting
🗃️ Tables & Data
Sort, filter, pivot tables
⚠️ Scoring System: You start with 20 points. You gain +4 points for each correct answer and lose 1 point for each wrong answer. Questions marked as "Don't Know" score 0. It is better not to guess.

⏱ Time Limit: You have 20 minutes to complete all 20 questions. You can skip questions and return to them later.
